# Unsupervised Usability Test Questionnaire / Cuestionario de Prueba de Usabilidad No Supervisada

## Introduction / Introduccion

**English**

Thank you for participating in this usability evaluation of Tessellum. The purpose of this questionnaire is to collect your opinion about the application, identify aspects that could be improved, detect features that do not work as expected, and understand which parts of the application are difficult to use or hard to understand.

This questionnaire is designed for an unsupervised usability test. Please answer based on your personal experience after exploring and using the application on your own.

Estimated completion time: 15-25 minutes.

Unless otherwise stated, use the following scale for rating questions:

- 1 = Strongly disagree
- 2 = Disagree
- 3 = Neither agree nor disagree
- 4 = Agree
- 5 = Strongly agree

**Espanol**

Gracias por participar en esta evaluacion de usabilidad de Tessellum. El objetivo de este cuestionario es recoger tu opinion sobre la aplicacion, identificar aspectos que podrian mejorarse, detectar funciones que no funcionan como se espera y conocer que partes de la aplicacion resultan dificiles de usar o de entender.
